Fast-Paced Hardware Based YSWS Like Hackpad


Welcome to Asylum! Asylum is a series of fast-paced hardware based YSWS in which you design a schematic for a project, turn it into a pcb, write code to power your device, and often design a case or enclosure to be 3D printed. Because these are so fast, they will often have different requirements and not all projects will include all of these.

If you want to get started right away you can go look at the Asylum repository or if you need some help you can take a look at the getting started section.
